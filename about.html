About

Our story

01

LOUWIETEC was founded on a simple observation: the enterprise software industry is built on a broken model.

Companies pay millions for systems they hope will work. Consultants bill thousands of hours with no guarantee of results. Vendors lock clients into multi-year contracts before delivering a single euro of value.

What if we took the risk instead?

What if we built the systems, deployed them, ran them — and only earned money when they actually delivered measurable savings? Not theoretical savings in a pitch deck. Real savings. Auditable. Transparent. Undeniable.

That question became LOUWIETEC.

Today, we build proprietary AI systems that we deploy directly into our clients' operations. We charge nothing upfront. We take a share of the value we create — and nothing more.

Our model aligns us completely with our clients. When they grow, we grow. When they save, we save. There is no scenario where we succeed and our client doesn't.

That's not a sales pitch. That's arithmetic.

“

We believe the next generation of enterprise technology will not be sold. It will be invested.

Software licenses, hourly billing, retainer agreements — these are relics of an era where technology was a cost center. In the world we're building, technology is an investment with measurable returns.

Every system we build is a bet on our client's success. We bet our engineering. We bet our infrastructure. We bet our time. And we only win when the numbers prove we were right.

This is not altruism. This is conviction. We build systems that work, and we're willing to prove it with our own economics.

Built for enterprise.

Proprietary infrastructure.

Every system runs on infrastructure we own and manage. No shared environments. No third-party dependencies your IT team needs to worry about. We handle security, uptime, and maintenance — you handle your business.

European data compliance.

All data processing happens within EU jurisdictions. Full GDPR compliance is not an add-on — it's the foundation. We build privacy into the architecture, not as an afterthought.

Seamless integration.

Our systems connect to your existing tools and workflows through secure APIs. No rip-and-replace. No disruption. We fit into your operations, not the other way around.

Continuous optimization.

Deployment is not the finish line — it's the starting point. Our systems learn, adapt, and improve continuously. Every month, the savings grow. That's the compound effect of AI done right.

Louis Wieshofer
